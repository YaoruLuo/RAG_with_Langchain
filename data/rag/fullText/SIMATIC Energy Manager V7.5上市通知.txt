从2024年5月9日开始，新版本能源管理系统-SIMATIC Energy Manager V7.5 将可以订货。 From May 9, 2024, the new version of SIMATIC Energy Manager V7.5 will be available to be ordered.

SIMATIC Energy Manager产品是用于全厂或企业集团级别的能源管理软件，通过简单的组态方式即可实现 工厂的能源（包括水，电，压缩空气等）数据可视化，以及专业的能源报表与能源消耗分析及预测等高级 功能。

SIMATIC Energy Manager is an energy management software for a plant-wide or group-wide energy management system that visualizes plant energy (water, electricity, compressed air, etc.) in a simple configuration way, as well as advanced functions such as professional energy reporting, energy consumption analysis and forecasting.

新版本Energy Manager V7.5新增诸如特性，如，新设计了“资产模型”来方便浏览各项信息；“目标驾驶舱” 用来定义能耗KPI目标及跟踪目标的完成情况；现代化风格的仪表板和能源图表与矩阵图以获得强大的能耗 数据分析能力；计算碳排放数据并交互到SiGREEN系统等。

The new version of Energy Manager V7.5 adds new features such as a newly designed "Asset Model" for easy access to information；"Target Cockpit" for defining energy KPIs and tracking of target achievement； modern dashboard and energy charts and matrix for powerful energy data analysis, and the calculation and interaction of carbon emissions data into the SiGREEN system.
